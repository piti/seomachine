## Linking Strategy

**Every article should include**:
- 1-2 links to Navigator (conversion priority)
- 2-3 links to related blog articles (topic cluster authority)
- 0-1 link to Strategy Audit (in conclusion, if appropriate)

**Anchor text principles**:
- Descriptive and keyword-rich
- Natural in sentence flow
- Variety (don't repeat same anchor text)
- Never generic ("click here" or "read more")
