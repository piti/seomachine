# Target Keywords for Coherent AI Content

This document lists prioritized keywords for Coherent AI blog articles, organized by theme and industry. These keywords target SMB decision-makers researching AI strategy and integration solutions.

**Last Updated**: November 10, 2025
**Target Audience**: SMB business owners, managing partners, operations directors ($5M-$50M revenue)
**Competitive Approach**: Long-tail keywords (3-5 words) emphasizing systems architecture differentiation

---

## Core Themes & Primary Keywords

### Theme 1: AI Sprawl & Strategic Planning
**Pillar Keywords**:
- `AI sprawl` (proprietary, low competition)
- `AI strategy for SMBs` (commercial intent, 1.2K/month)
- `business AI strategy consulting` (high intent, 800/month)

**Long-Tail Keywords**:
- `why AI tools don't work together`
- `too many AI subscriptions business`
- `AI implementation not delivering ROI`
- `how to connect disconnected AI tools`

### Theme 2: AI Integration & Architecture
**Pillar Keywords**:
- `AI integration strategy` (1.5K/month)
- `business AI integration` (1K/month)
- `AI systems integration` (medium competition)

**Long-Tail Keywords**:
- `integrate AI tools business`
- `AI workflow integration challenges`
- `unified AI platform architecture`

### Theme 3: AI ROI & Measurement
**Pillar Keywords**:
- `AI ROI measurement` (2K/month, high intent)
- `measure AI return on investment` (commercial)
- `prove AI ROI executives`

**Long-Tail Keywords**:
- `calculate AI ROI SMB`
- `AI business case justification`
- `measure AI strategic value`
